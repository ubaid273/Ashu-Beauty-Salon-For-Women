Welcome to Ashu Beauty Salon for Women

We provide the best beauty treatments for women with a focus on quality, relaxation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Services for Women

Hair Services

Our hair services are designed to help you look your best with a variety of options to suit your needs. Whether you're looking for a simple haircut or a complete transformation, our expert stylists are here to give you a look you'll love.

  • Haircut: Tailored to suit your face shape and style preferences.
  • Stepcut: A modern haircut technique to add layers and volume.
  • Staining: Enhance the depth and shine of your natural hair color.
  • Coloring Striking: Bold and bright hair colors for a dramatic new look.
  • Keratin Treatment: Smooth and straighten your hair for long-lasting results.
  • Hair Remonding: A chemical process to straighten and maintain sleek hair.
  • Hair Styling: Get ready for any event with professional styling services.

Eye Care

Your eyes are the windows to your soul, and we take special care to highlight them. From eyebrow shaping to upper lip treatment, we provide services that enhance your natural beauty.

  • Eyebrow Shaping: Expert shaping to enhance the natural curve of your brows.
  • Upper Lip For Hade: Smooth away unwanted hair for a flawless finish.

Waxing Services

Achieve smooth and silky skin with our professional waxing services. Whether it's for your face, hands, legs, or full body, we use high-quality wax that ensures a painless experience.

  • Face Wax: Remove unwanted facial hair for a clear and smooth face.
  • Hand & Leg Wax: Achieve soft, hair-free hands and legs.
  • Full Body Wax: Enjoy hair-free skin from head to toe with our full body waxing service.

Makeup Services

Our makeup services are perfect for every occasion, from a casual party to your big day. Let our expert makeup artists help you feel beautiful and confident.

  • Party Makeup: Get the perfect look for any special occasion.
  • Model Makeup: Professional makeup for photo shoots and runway events.
  • Mehndi Makeup: Elegant makeup to complement your traditional outfit.
  • Nikah Makeup: Beautiful and long-lasting makeup for your wedding ceremony.
  • Bridal Makeup: Perfect bridal look with personalized makeup that lasts all day.
  • Walima Makeup: Gorgeous makeup for your Walima ceremony or reception.

Facial Services

Our facial treatments are designed to rejuvenate your skin and give you a healthy glow. Whether you need a relaxing facial or a treatment for specific skin concerns, we have the right option for you.

  • Manicure & Pedicure: Pamper your hands and feet with our luxurious treatments.
  • Bleach: Lighten your skin tone and remove blemishes for a radiant look.
  • Skin Polish: Exfoliate and remove dead skin cells for smooth skin.
  • Facial: A soothing facial to cleanse and hydrate your skin.
  • Facial Sheet Mask: Deep nourishment with a sheet mask for glowing skin.
  • Hydra Facial: A non-invasive facial treatment that deeply cleanses, hydrates, and refreshes the skin.
  • Face Cleansing: Gentle cleansing to refresh your face and remove impurities.
